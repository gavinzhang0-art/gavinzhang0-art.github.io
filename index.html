QWhat types of clients do you work with?
Primarily individuals, private investors, and small business owners in the GTA seeking financial clarity on investment decisions, asset management, revenue optimization, or financial operations. Both one-time sessions and ongoing engagements are available.
QDo I need to be in Toronto to work with you?
No. Consultations are available in-person (Toronto/Markham area) or by video call. Many clients are fully remote. All written deliverables are digital and can be shared securely regardless of location.
QHow is the pricing structured?
All pricing is disclosed upfront — $150 for a single session, $450 for a 30-day engagement, and $1,200 for a comprehensive 60-day advisory. No hidden fees, no ongoing retainers unless specifically agreed upon.
QWhat makes your approach different?
Every recommendation is grounded in quantitative analysis — regression models, financial modeling, market data. Every engagement produces a written deliverable. Results are documented and measurable, not just advisory opinions.
QAre you a licensed financial advisor?
Currently pursuing the CSC and LLQP. Engagements focus on consulting, strategic advisory, and financial analysis. Clients are encouraged to consult licensed professionals for regulated financial products.
QHow quickly can we start?
Tier 01 can often be scheduled within the week. Tier 02 and Tier 03 engagements typically start within 1–2 weeks of scope confirmation. Reach out and a response follows within 24 hours.
