How It Works

From first call to financial plan — three clear steps.

No pressure. No jargon. Just a straightforward conversation about where you are, where you want to go, and how Sun Life's solutions can get you there.

01

Free Consultation

A 30-minute call or meeting, in person or by video. Tell me about your situation, your goals, and your timeline. No obligation whatsoever — this step is purely about understanding where you're at.

02

Needs Analysis & Recommendation

I assess your full financial picture — income, family, debts, goals — and build a tailored plan from Sun Life's product suite. Every recommendation is explained clearly, in plain language, before any decision is made.

03

Ongoing Support

Annual reviews, life event check-ins, and a direct line whenever you have questions. A relationship that grows with you, not a one-time transaction.
